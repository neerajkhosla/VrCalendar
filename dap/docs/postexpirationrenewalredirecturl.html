This is the URL to which a user is redirected to, right after they log in, if MORE THAN ONE of the products that they have access to, have expired.

If access to ONLY ONE of the products has expired, DAP will take the Post-Expiry Renewal Redirect URL at the Product level.

WARNING: For renewal redirection to take effect, you must also set "Redirect Expired Users to Renewal Page Upon Login?" to "Y".
