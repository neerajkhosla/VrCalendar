This is in addition to the initial order or trial (if there's a trial)

Set the "Recurring Count" in DAP only if...

1) You are selling via Paypal, and are going to be using DAP-generated Buy Buttons

2) You are selling via Authorize.net or Paypal Website Payments Pro

You DO NOT have to enter anything in this "Recurring Count" field in DAP if...

1) You are selling via Paypal, BUT are going to be using Paypal-hosted Buy Buttons (generated from within your Paypal account)

2) You are selling via any of the other supported external carts like:
1ShoppingCart, 1SiteAutomation, e-Junkie, etc.

So in this case, you can just leave the price at whatever price you want. DAP will ignore it. DAP will blindly accept the price provided by the external cart or payment processor you're using.

Example settings:

* No trial, monthly recurring, keep charging until customer cancels (Unlimited-recurring)
Set to 9999

* No trial, 6 total payments in TOTAL
Set to 5
(because the initial order will be 1, plus 5 recurring. So 1 (today) + 5 (Recurring) = 6 Payments in TOTAL

* 30-day trial, only 6 total payments in TOTAL including trial
Set to 5
(because the initial trial will be counted as 1, plus 5 recurring. So 1 (today) + 5 (Recurring) = 6 Payments in TOTAL

* 7-day trial, followed by 6 monthly payments
Set to 6

* 30-day trial followed by 12 payments
Set to 12

* 30-day trial followed by 11 payments
