Default: /dap/unsubscribed.php

This is the URL to which a user is redirected to, right after they click on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of your emails sent via DAP.

TIP: You can automatically insert an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of every autoresponder, broadcast and welcome email sent by DAP, simply by inserting the text %%UNSUB_LINK%% as the last line of your email body.
