Murray Hill, New York

The Murray Hill Neighborhood is the southern plot of Midtown, located on the East Side but not all the way to the East River. It is the East Side's equivalent of the Garment District. The neighborhood technically runs from 29th to 42nd Street and from 2nd to 5th Avenue.

Murray Hill varies in composition based on how far North or South one goes. At the northern end, huge glass and steel office towers dominate the picture, just South of Grand Central Station. As you go South
